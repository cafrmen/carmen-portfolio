Granulator

Granulation is a process in which an audio sample is divided into small audio segments. Created by Fabian Campuzano within Max/MSP, based on the C programming language. Max is named after synthesis pioneer Max Mathews, who first demonstrated music synthesis on a digital computer in 1957. It was exported to a JSON file and it was finally designed with JS and CSS.

CFR Nasa API Post

A newspaper where you can find out what the astronomy picture of the day is, the weekly space weather report, what asteroids will pass near the earth today and if any were dangerous and finally you can listen to some audios, all with NASA material thanks to its API.
